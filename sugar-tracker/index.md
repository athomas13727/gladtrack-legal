# Privacy Policy for Sugar Tracker by GladTrack

**Effective Date:** July 10, 2026
**Last Updated:** July 11, 2026

Aaron Thomas ("we," "our," or "us") built Sugar Tracker by GladTrack ("the App") as a paid-unlock app with no subscriptions or ads. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**We do not collect any personal information.**

- The App does not require an account, sign-in, or profile of any kind.
- The App works fully offline. There is no backend server, and the App does not send your logged food, sugar totals, or device data anywhere.
- We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking technologies of any kind.
- Everything you log (foods, amounts, goals, favorites) is stored only in local storage on your own device, and is never transmitted to us or anyone else.

### Camera
The App can use your camera to take a photo of food so it can estimate the sugar content. Photos are processed in the moment to produce an estimate and are not stored, saved, or uploaded anywhere by the App.

### Microphone & Speech Recognition
The App can use your microphone so you can dictate what you ate instead of typing. Speech recognition runs on-device whenever your device supports it, meaning the audio never leaves your device. On older devices that don't support on-device recognition, Apple's speech recognition service may process the audio instead — in that case, audio is sent to Apple to produce a transcription and is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/), not this one.

### Apple Health
The App includes an optional, off-by-default setting to sync the sugar you log to Apple Health. If you turn this on, the App writes dietary sugar entries to Health so they appear alongside your other nutrition data; if you turn it off (or never turn it on), no data is shared with Health at all. Any data written to Health is governed by Apple's own Health privacy protections, not this policy.

### Barcode Scanning
The App can look up a scanned barcode against the free, public Open Food Facts database to retrieve nutrition information for packaged food. The barcode itself is sent to Open Food Facts to perform the lookup; no other information about you or your device is sent along with it.

### Goal Suggestion Helper
The App includes an optional "suggest a goal for me" helper that takes height, weight, age, sex, and a diet-style choice you enter directly into the sheet to compute a starting-point daily sugar goal. This information is used only for that one-time calculation on your device — it is never stored, saved, or transmitted anywhere, and disappears the moment you leave the sheet unless you tap "Use This Goal" (which saves only the resulting gram number, not the inputs).

## 2. In-App Purchases

The App offers a single one-time purchase ("Unlock Forever," $4.99) that unlocks the App after a 7-day free trial. This purchase is processed entirely through Apple's App Store using Apple's standard in-app purchase system (StoreKit). We do not use any third-party purchase-verification service — Apple's App Store is the only party involved in processing and verifying your purchase.

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|-----------------|
| Apple App Store / StoreKit | Processes the in-app purchase and payment | [Apple Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/privacy/) |
| Open Food Facts | Looks up nutrition info for a scanned barcode | [Open Food Facts Privacy Policy](https://world.openfoodfacts.org/privacy) |
