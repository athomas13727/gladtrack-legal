# Support — Fasting Timer by GladTrack

Thanks for using Fasting Timer.

## Common questions

**How does the free trial work?**  
You get 7 days of full access after finishing onboarding. Then a single one-time unlock (no subscription) keeps the app forever.

**Is there a subscription?**  
No. One-time unlock only.

**Where is my data stored?**  
On your iPhone (and shared with the widget via an App Group). There is no GladTrack cloud account and no iCloud sync in this app.

**Can I use custom plan lengths?**  
Yes — Custom lets you pick any window from 1–72 hours.

**Does it work offline?**  
Yes. Timer, history, and streaks work fully offline.

**Apple Health**  
Optional. When enabled, completed fasts are written as mindful sessions.
