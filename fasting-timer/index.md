# Privacy Policy — Fasting Timer by GladTrack

**Last updated:** 2026-07-12

Fasting Timer is a simple intermittent-fasting timer. Your privacy matters.

## What we collect

- **Nothing from our servers.** There is no GladTrack account and no cloud backend for this app.
- **On your device only:** fasting sessions, plan preferences, reminder settings, and purchase entitlement state are stored locally (SwiftData / App Group container for widgets).
- **No iCloud sync.** Data does not leave your device via GladTrack infrastructure.
- **Optional Apple Health:** if you enable Health sync, completed fasts may be written as mindful sessions through Apple’s HealthKit APIs. That data is governed by Apple’s Health privacy model.
- **Purchases:** lifetime unlock is processed by Apple via StoreKit. We do not receive your payment card details.

## What we don’t do

- No ads
- No analytics SDKs that track you across apps
- No selling of personal data
- No forced account or sign-in
