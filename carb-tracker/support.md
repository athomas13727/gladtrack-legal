# Carb Tracker by GladTrack - Support Page

**Contact & Response Time**
The developer can be reached at athomas13727@gmail.com, with a typical response window of a few business days.

**No Account Required**
Carb Tracker works entirely offline and stores everything locally on your device. There's no sign-in, registration, or internet connection required to log food or see your progress.

**Free Trial & Unlocking**
The App includes a 7-day free trial with full access to every feature. After the trial, a one-time $4.99 purchase unlocks the App forever — there's no subscription and no recurring charge.

**Restoring Your Purchase**
If you've already unlocked the App on another device, or after reinstalling, use "Restore Purchase" in Settings while signed in with the same Apple ID.

**Logging Food**
You can log what you ate several ways:
- Type it (e.g. "1 bagel" or "40g carbs")
- Dictate it — tap the microphone and speak; it automatically stops listening and logs after a couple seconds of silence
- Take a photo for an instant estimate
- Scan a barcode for exact packaged nutrition
- Enter an exact gram amount manually if you already know the number

**Editing or Removing an Entry**
Swipe left on any item in "Logged Today" to Edit or Delete it.

**Favorites & Quick Add**
Star any entry to pin it as a favorite for one-tap re-logging. Long-press a Quick Add item to remove it from favorites.

**Home Screen Widgets**
Add the Carb Tracker widget to your Home Screen to see today's progress and log a favorite food without opening the App.

**Apple Watch**
The companion Watch app shows your progress and lets you log food from your wrist.

**Apple Health**
Turn on "Sync to Health" in Settings to have logged carbs written to Apple Health automatically. This is off by default.

**Siri**
Say "Hey Siri, log carbs in Carb Tracker" to log hands-free.

**Daily Goal**
Set your daily carb goal in Settings, or adjust it just for today by tapping the ring on the Today screen (if "same goal every day" is turned off). Not sure where to start? The goal step includes a "suggest one for me" helper that offers a starting-point estimate for a General or Low-carb/Keto diet style, which you can fine-tune from there.
