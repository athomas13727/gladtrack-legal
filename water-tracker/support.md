# Water Tracker by GladTrack - Support Page

**Contact & Response Time**  
The developer can be reached at GladTrack.support@gmail.com, with a typical response window of a few business days.

**No Account Required**  
Water Tracker works entirely offline and stores everything locally on your device. There's no sign-in, registration, or internet connection required to log water or see your progress.

**Free Trial & Unlocking**  
The App includes a 7-day free trial with full access to every feature. After the trial, a one-time $4.99 purchase unlocks the App forever — there's no subscription and no recurring charge.

**Restoring Your Purchase**  
If you've already unlocked the App on another device, or after reinstalling, use "Restore Purchase" in Settings while signed in with the same Apple ID.

**Units**  
Track in metric (ml / L) or US fluid ounces. Your device region sets a sensible default (US → fl oz; elsewhere → metric). Change units anytime in Settings; amounts are stored the same way either way.

**Logging Water**  
You can log intake several ways:
- Tap a quick volume chip (glass/bottle sizes in your chosen units)
- Type it (e.g. "glass", "500 ml", or "16 oz")
- Dictate it — tap the microphone and speak; it stops listening and can log after a short pause
- Enter a custom amount manually if you already know the number

**Editing or Removing an Entry**  
Swipe left on any item in "Logged Today" to Edit or Delete it.

**Favorites & Quick Add**  
Star any entry to pin it as a favorite for one-tap re-logging. Long-press a Quick Add item to add or remove it from favorites.

**Home Screen Widgets**  
Add a Water Tracker widget to your Home Screen to see today's progress and log a volume or favorite without opening the App.

**Apple Watch**  
The companion Watch app shows today's progress and lets you log water from your wrist (quick volumes, voice, and favorites when synced).

**Apple Health**  
Turn on "Sync to Health" in Settings to have logged water written to Apple Health automatically. This is off by default.

**Siri**  
Say "Hey Siri, log water in Water Tracker" to log hands-free.

**Daily Goal**  
Set your daily water goal in Settings, or adjust it just for today by tapping the ring on the Today screen (if "same goal every day" is turned off). Not sure where to start? Onboarding and Settings include a suggestion helper for a starting-point estimate you can fine-tune.
